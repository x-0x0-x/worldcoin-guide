## 🛠 Step-by-Step Implementation

### 1. Account Initialization
1. Download the **World App** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/world-app/id1531061480) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.worldcoin.app).
2. Follow the on-screen instructions for phone number/email verification.
3. **Crucial:** Go to `Settings` > `Referrals` and enter code: **`EIUR31H`**.

### 2. World ID & Orb Verification
To fully participate in the Worldcoin economy (WLD Grants), you must verify your personhood via an **Orb**.

- **What is an Orb?** A biometric imaging device that creates a unique IrisCode to ensure sybil-resistance.
- **Find a Location:** Use the "Find an Orb" map in the app. Major hubs include Tokyo, Seoul, London, and Taipei.
- **Privacy Note:** The Orb does not store your iris images by default; it only generates a zero-knowledge proof (ZKP).

### 3. Claiming WLD Grants
Once verified, you are eligible for periodic WLD token grants.
- **Frequency:** Usually every 2 weeks.
- **Network:** Optimism (OP) Mainnet.
- **Gas:** World App provides gas-less transactions for verified users.
